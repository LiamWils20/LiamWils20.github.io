Alone, Scared, Eager for Revenge



Meet Chub, a human transformed into a lab rat by a morally questionable alchemist. Tired of the cruel fate bestowed upon him, Chub has managed to break free from his cage and is ready to exact his revenge. Oh one thing though, he is not the brevest of fellows..


- Concept by Winnie Au


In this 2.5D puzzle adventure, play as Chub as he attempts to cause mild inconveniences for the alchemist. But there is a catch, his antics must be enacted from the shadows and out of sight, lest he wishes to return to a life of despair.


Utilise the environment to create pathways leading towards the philosophers stone. Rearranging the alchemy table's contents, temporarily foiling hiscaptors experiments. Along the way Chub may even learn some helpful tricks of his own!


Will Chub be able to cause enough chaos and frustration to qualm his thirst for vengeance and finally escape once and for all?



Controls



WASD Movement
WASD + Space Pull / Push Objects
LMB Farm Interaction (When Looking at the farm)
E Interact With Objects
1 2 3 Activate Potions
