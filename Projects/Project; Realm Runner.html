    About

    Realm Runner is a platforming-action-puzzle adventure where
    you will explore different dimension to retrieve lost fragments,
    the source of life in this universe. Hurry! You must save this
    reality from the threat lurking within the dark.


    Traverse your way through different realms of the universe,
    using your skills of platforming, solving puzzles and defeating
    enemies. Doing all this whilst collecting coins and fragments to
    progress through the realms right up until the final boss

    • My Duty; UI Programmer

    This section is a detailed explanation of what I did as the UI programmer, on this project. At the end, there will be a section of things I did which got cut or postponed due to scope and time remaining.


    Menus

    My first obligation for this project was creating the main menu. Everything regarding the Main Menu went into one singular script, which I no longer do but it's what I did at the time of this project. For every button I had on the main menu, I created a function, each funtion then ran the buttons specific needs for example, the "Start Game" button, had the code line "Scene.Manager.LoadScene("HubLevelWithAssets");". Each button had their own unique funtion, I won't go through them all as most are self explanatory.
