    About


    Realm Runner is a platforming-action-puzzle adventure where
    you will explore different dimension to retrieve lost fragments,
    the source of life in this universe. Hurry! You must save this
    reality from the threat lurking within the dark.


    Traverse your way through different realms of the universe,
    using your skills of platforming, solving puzzles and defeating
    enemies. Doing all this whilst collecting coins and fragments to
    progress through the realms right up until the final boss



    My Contributions, in detail


    Baseline

    This project was made as a university project within a 13 week time frame. We were given teams to work amongst, and was tasked with the creation of a game, which was based on either Super Mario 64, or Crash Bandicoot. Our team chose Super Mario 64.


    You can view the rest of the team by scrolling further, or click here to be transported there.


    Within these 13 weeks, we each had many tasks we were require to acomplish mine were as follows;

  • UI Programmer - Menus, Health, Fragment Collection etc
  • Combat System - Bow & Arrow - Attack Mechanics
  • Sound & World Objects Programmer
  • Animations
