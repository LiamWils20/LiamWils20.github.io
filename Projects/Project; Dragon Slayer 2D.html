A 2D Adventure of Revenge, Courage, and Fire.


Your family is gone. Burned to ash by the fury of the dragons.
All that remains is you - and the steal in your hand


Step into the shoes of the lone warrior on a quest for vengeance in Dragon Slayer, a 2D pixel-style adventure where survival means courage, and every swing of your blade brings you closer to justice.



The Project

Dragon Slayer 2D, was designed and developed as a learning project for me to explore and understand 2D development within Godot, a new engine to me and therefore my desire to learn the engine got the better of me causing me to study up and learn what I can



Features

  • Epic 2D Adventure: Explore dangerous lands filled with deadly dragons trecherous, and hidden secrets.
  • Fast-Paced Combat: Wield your sword against dragons and monsters in intense, skill-based battles.
  • A Story of Revenge: Driven by loss, your journey is personal. Will vengeance heal your wounds - or consume you?
  • Pixel Art World: Hand-crafted pixel environments and creatures being nostalgic yet fresh visual style.
  • Challenging Boss Fights: Test your skills against powerful dragons that each demand a different strategy.
  • Atmospheric Soundtrack: Feel the tension rise as the music builds with every step of your journey.
