About



When the hunter becomes the hunted the world turns to war. In this face paced runner game you must flee the grasp of the monster and return to your friends, in order to enact your plan.



Project Backstory



Risen from Hell was created as a part of the ScoreSpace Jam #29 game jam. Where it placed 66th, results below.




I built this game because the theme was "Escape", and I thought a hunter who is being hunted by the monster in a dark horror stylised environment would be cool. So, with that in mind I got to work in building the game.



How to Play?



Spawn in and RUN!!
We highly suggest you comply, otherwise you may become lunch and we do not want that do we? Run down and around the corridoor, avoiding obstacles by dodging or lunging over them. With speed, agility and skill you can avoid capture, and avoid being lunch.



Controls



WASD Movement
Left Shift Sprint
Space Jump
Escape Pause
Left Shift Sprint
