The Project



Farm, Defend and Build your very own fortress. Defend yourself and crops at all costs, welcome to Kade's Last Stand. A world where you can fight your way through hordes of mutated souls, whilst farming and expanding your net worth in order to build, upgrade and buy weapons and defences.



Project Backstory



This here is a game developed for and during the 2024 Fall Cougar Cup. It is a project, which we have always wanted to develop and this ham incentivised us to produce such a project. We honestly hope you enjoy the game, and if you do not please tell us why. We always love to recieve feedback to help improve our games.

    About


    Neon Racer is a collaborative project between SAE Students and LiminalVR where we create an energising experience for the LiminalVR platform. We have programmed an experience that allows the player to feel energised through the use of audio and various gameplay mechanics.
