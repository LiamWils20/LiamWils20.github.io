Project Outline



Realm Runner is a project developed as part of an educational course conducted by SAE Insitute. A team of 5 students from SAE was grouped together and required to create a video game. The game had to be made in the style of either Crash Bandicoot, or Super Mario 64. My team decided, to create a game in the style of Super Mario 64.


The game's development was 13 weeks in total. Meaning 13 weeks, for planning / design, pre-production, prototype, testing and then release. Not a lot of time is it? My responisbilities are listed above, but I'll list them again here.

- UI Programmer, Main Menu Scene and Menus Designer
- Bow and Arrow Attack Programmer
- Sound & World Objects Programmer
- Animations & Death Rigging


For these contribution entries I aim to go into great detail explaning everything I did.

About


Realm Runner is a platforming-action-puzzle adventure where
you will explore different dimension to retrieve lost fragments,
the source of life in this universe. Hurry! You must save this
reality from the threat lurking within the dark.


Traverse your way through different realms of the universe,
using your skills of platforming, solving puzzles and defeating
enemies. Doing all this whilst collecting coins and fragments to
progress through the realms right up until the final boss
