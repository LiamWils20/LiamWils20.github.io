Development is cancelled


BuildABang is a project where you can build your frustrations a way. Build freely with buildings, roads, and props you have at your disposal. And most importantly enjoy.



Project Backstory



BuildABang was built as an end of study graduate project. The project was built to answer a research question, the question was somethine along the lines of "In terms of player experience and interaction which UI design is best to keep players engaged during a survival games build system". This is not the exact question, I do not remember nor do I know where the exact question is located.



Controls



WASD Movement
Left Shift Sprint
C/CTRL Descend / Down
TAB Open / Close Build Menu
LMB Place Build
MMB Rotate
Mouse Look Around
Escape Open / Close Pause Menu
P Enable / Disable Picture Mode
O Take Picture (Picture Mode Only)
RMB UI Back (Scene 2 Only)
