Project Plans

New Projects

It's been a while since I have posted a blog update, for this I do apologise.

I have begun working on some website based projects, a couple of them are done in terms of basic functionality others require more work.

The next few titles will be of these new projects, what they are, why I decided to make em.

VoidTimer, Link, is a countdown based website for those who wish to countdown to a special event i.e. a holiday, but you don't wish to actually countdown.

GeoBlade, Link, is a simple virtual table-top, designed for online tabletop role-playing games (RPGs). It allows users to upload and play their own maps, with future plans to add features such as dice rolling, character sheets, and other tools to enhance the gaming experience.

MysteryBox, Link, is a unique platform that offers a variety of mystery box experiences for users. Each box contains a curated selection of items based on user preferences, making it a fun and personalized surprise.

First Post Welcome

Hey everyone, and welcome to the first official post on my game development blog! I'm super excited to share my journey with you as I build games, tackling challenges, and creating awesome experiences. This blog will serve as a hub for updates, behind-the-scenes looks, and insights into my development and learning processes. Let’s dive in!


Building the Portfolio Website

One of the first steps in making my game development journey more official was creating this website. I wanted a space where I could share my progress, provide updates, and showcase my work to the you, and the community. It took some time to get everything set up, but I'm happy with how it’s coming along. Expect more improvements and features in the future, including dedicated sections for my projects, dev logs, and possibly even a forum for discussion!

Currently, my primary focus is on increasing my knowledge with engines such as Unity & Unreal, I plan to delve into C# and C++ programming languages, and I'm excited to see where this journey takes me.
