About

Meet Chub, a human transformed into a lab rat by a morally questionable alchemist. Tired of the cruel fate bestowed upon him, Chub has managed to break free from his cage and is ready to exact his revenge. Oh one thing though, he is not the bravest of fellows.


- Concept art by Winnie Au


In this 2.5D puzzle adventure, play as Chub as he attempts to cause mild inconveniences for the alchemist. But there is a catch, hist antics must be enacted from the shadows and out of sight, lest he wishes to return to a life of despair.


Utilise the environment to create pathways leading towards the philosopher's stone. Rearranging the alchemy table's contents, temporarily foiling his captors experimnets. Along the way Chub may even learn some helpful tricks of his own!


Will Chub be able to cause enough chaos and frustration to qualm his thirst for vengeance and finally escape once and for all?

Project Backstory

Chub's Revenge is a collaborative project created within Unity and as a part of the Pirate Software - Game Jam 15 For more details about persons involved and their contributions, check out the team section below, or the my contributions section.


Realm Runner was created as a university project within a 13 week timeframe. It is no longer in-development, however we may one day return to it if the stars align.

Keyboard Controls

  • WASD - Player Movemennt [Up, Left, Down, Right]
  • WASD + Space - Pull / Push Movable Objects
  • E - Interact with Objects
  • 1 2 3 - Activate Potions [Potion 1, Potion 2, Potion 3]
