About

Dragon Slayer is a 2D Adventure of revenge, courage, and fire.


Your family is gone. Burned to ash by the fury of the dragons.
All that remains is you - and the steal in your hand.


Step into the shoes of the lone warrior on a quest for vengeance in Dragon Slayer, a 2D pixel-style adventure where survival means courage, and every swing of your blade brings you closer to justice.

Project Backstory

Dragon Slayer, was designed and developed as a learning project for me to explore and understand 2D development within the Godot Engine, which is a new engine to me and therefore my desire to learn the engine got the better of me causing me to study up and learn what I can.
