About

BuildABang is a 3D project where you can build your frustrations away. Build freely with buildings, roads and props you have at your disposal. And most importantly enjoy.

Project Backstory

BuildABang was built as an end of study graduate project.

The project was built to aid the answering of a research question, the question was something along the lines of "in terms of player experience and interaction which UI design is best to keep players engaged during a survival games build system".


For more details about persons involved and their contributions, check out the team section below, or the my contributions section.

Keyboard Controls

  • WASD → Move
  • Left SHift → Sprint
  • Space → Fly Up
  • C/CTRL → Fly Down
  • TAB → Toggle Build Menu
  • LMB → Place Build
  • MMB → Rotate Build
  • Mouse → Look Around
  • ESC → Toggle Pause Menu
  • P → Toggle Picture Mode
  • O → Take Picture (Picture Mode Only)
  • RMB → UI Back (Scene 2 Only)
