About

In this fast-paced world you must race your way to the finish. You must refrain from hitting any of the debuffs, but collect as many buffs as humanly possible. The more you collect the more score you obtain, it's necessary for your come out with the highest score.

Project Backstory

Neon Racer is a collaborative project between four SAE students and LiminalVR where we create an energising experience for the LiminalVR's platform. We have programmed an experience that allows the player to feel energised through the use of audio and various gameplay mechanics.
