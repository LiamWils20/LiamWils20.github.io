About

When the hunter becomes the hunted the world turns to war. In this fast-paced 3D runner, you must flee the grasp of the vial monster and return to your friends, in order to enact your plan.

Project Backstory

Risen From Hell was created as a part of the ScoreSpace Jam #29 game jam. Where it placed 66th, results below.
