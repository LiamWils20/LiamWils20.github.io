About

Farm, Defend and Build your very own fortress. Defend yourself and your crops at all costs.

Welcome to Kade's Last Stand!

A world where you can fight your way through hordes of mutated souls, whilst farming and expanding your net worth in order to build, upgrade and buy weapons and defences.

Project Backstory

Kade's Last Stand was developed for and during the 2024 Fall Cougar Cup.

It was constructed within a 7 day time frame, with a theme of "HARVEST", so I opted for a wave based farming game where the player needs to farm inorder to make money to buy defences and weapons to defend themselves from waves of enemies.

Keyboard Controls

  • WASD → Move
  • TAB → Inventory
  • LMB → Interact with Farm
  • LMB → Attacking
  • Left Shift → Run
  • E → NPC Interaction
  • Escape → Toggle Pause
