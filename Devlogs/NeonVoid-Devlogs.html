NeonVoid!


First Post


Friday 23rd May 2025


First off welcome too the NeonVoid game devlog. Secondly, this log entry will be an introductory log for the idea, and plan of action regarding this game.


The Idea

The idea is an Android and IOS, sci-fi runner game similar to Subway Surfer, Temple Run and Minion Rush mobile titles.

The player will be a spacecraft traversing through a neon city environment.

This project will be a solo project, and will heavily rely on already made assets by 3rd party creators. I will give credit where credit is due.


Later down the line though, I may convert the assets used within the game to be custom assets made by me or perhaps by a skilled artist, but until then it will be pre-made assets.


The Plan of Action

For this project, I will begin with creating the player features, and movement, then the terrain will be my next target. Every Friday I will release a devlog and video log. Devlogs will be posted here, and videologs will be posted on the Wi.Bi Studios YouTube channel, so stay tuned for that. I will also include gifs, pngs and jpegs for the written logs, so that it's not just words as I'm aware that would be boring and possibly deter people from reading.
